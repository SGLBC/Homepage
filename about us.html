Our Belief

The Scriptures

The Holy Bible was written by men divinely inspired and is the record of God's revelation of Himself to man. It is a perfect treasure of divine instructions and has God for its author, salvation for its end, and truth, without any mixture of error, for its matter.

God

There is one and only one living and true God. Jesus Christ is the eternal Son of God. He was conceived of the Holy Spirit and born of the virgin Mary. Jesus revealed and did the will of God and in His death on the cross, he made provision for the redemption of men from sin.He was raised from the dead with a glorified body, appeared to many after his resurrection, and ascended into heaven and is now sitting at the right hand of the throne of God. He lives in all believers through the Holy Spirit of God, the third person of the Trinity.

Salvation

Salvation involves the redemption of the whole man and is offered freely to all who accept Jesus Christ as Lord and Saviour. In its broadest sense, salvation includes regeneration, sanctification, and glorification. All true believers endure to the end. Those whom God has accepted in Christ, and are sanctified by His Spirit, will never fall away from the state of grace, but shall persevere to the end.

Mission Statement

SGLBC is a fellowship of believers that purpose to know God through a personal relationship with Jesus Christ, to equip believers through discipleship. Show God’s love, we strive to reach a hungry world with Jesus Chrsit through obedience to the Great Commission.

Ministries

Our ministries are an important and integral part of our church community. Whether you are a student, single, married or married with children there is a ministry designed for you. Please read about our ministries and consider becoming an active member of our church family.



Children’s Ministry 5th Grade and Below-

The Primary Objective of our Children's Ministry is to share the Love of Jesus in a safe and caring environment. We use a curriculum which is age appropriate with an emphasis on Jesus as God's Son. Children are taught to pray, to read their bibles, to memorize bible verses, and also to give, serve, and trust God's Holy Word, the Bible to be fully true. We share the plan of salvation and we trust the Holy Spirit will draw them to salvation in Christ. We can provide transportation for those who request a ride to our services.


Family Night Wednesday

Join us for dinner at 5:45 PM and Bible study at 6:30PM for all ages.


Monthly Women’s Fellowship

The purpose of our Women's ministry is to encourage women of every age to grow in our relationship with God, connect to sisters in the faith and provide opportunities to display God’s Love as we serve one another and our community for the Glory of God.
