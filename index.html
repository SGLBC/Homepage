About SGLBC

SGLBC is a fellowship of believers that purpose to know God through a personal relationship with Jesus Christ, to equip believers through discipleship. Show God’s love, we strive to reach a hungry world with Jesus Chrsit through obedience to the Great Commission.

Womens Fellowship

Monthly Women’s Fellowship - The purpose of our Women's ministry is to encourage women of every age to grow in our relationship with God, connect to sisters in the faith and provide opportunities to display God’s Love as we serve one another and our community for the Glory of God.
